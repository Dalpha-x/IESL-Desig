About Michharry & Company Nigeria Ltd


WHY MICHHARRY & COMPANY NIGERIA LTD

Michharry & Company (Nig.) Limited (MCNL) has been a foremost provider of offshore and onshore facilities for the oil and gas industry since 1992, having worked directly with Oil companies operating in Nigeria as a main contractor and also International Oil companies as a sub-contractor. We are also into marine vessels and equipment supply services and environmental laboratory consultancy.

Our services are backed by cutting-edge technology, strict adherence to international and national standards, and a highly experienced team with over 200 years of combined management experience. We prioritize safety, innovation, integrity, and operational excellence—delivering impactful results across Africa’s energy landscape.

At MCNL, we are committed to sustainable practices and continuous innovation—ensuring our clients receive exceptional value, performance, and reliability in every project.

Chief Dr. Michael Edematie-Ikuku – Founder & CEO

With over 20 years of experience in engineering and energy services, Chief Dr. Michael Edematie-Ikuku founded Michharry & Company Nigeria Ltd with a vision to deliver excellence in infrastructure, construction, and technical innovation. His leadership continues to guide the company towards growth and impact.

What We Do

Marine Vessels & Equipment Supply

We own and operate specialized vessels, including liftboats, supply vessels, and jack-up barges, to support offshore and onshore projects.

Engineering, Procurement, Installation & Construction (EPIC)

We deliver turnkey engineering solutions covering mechanical, electrical, civil, and instrumentation works.

Operations & Maintenance

Through our well-equipped workshop and expert teams, we provide maintenance for critical equipment to ensure reliability and performance.

Environmental & Laboratory Services

We offer environmental consultancy and analytical laboratory services, ensuring compliance with industry standards while promoting sustainability.

Project Execution Across Terrains

From land and swamp to offshore and deep-water operations, we bring efficiency, safety, and innovation to every project.
